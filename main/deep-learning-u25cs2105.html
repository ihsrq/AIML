Weekly Learning Goals

  • Understand neural network fundamentals: activation functions, backpropagation, and gradient descent.
  • Master deep learning frameworks: TensorFlow and PyTorch implementation.
  • Explore CNN architectures for image processing and computer vision tasks.
  • Study RNNs, LSTMs, and Transformers for sequence modeling and NLP.
  • Learn transfer learning, fine-tuning, and model deployment strategies.
